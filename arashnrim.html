Here's the history.

Looking at many developer portfolios over the years, I grew in interest in wanting to have one for myself as well. Although it doesn't have much of a use case for now, it's always nice to have a project that showcases yourself and is, in and of itself, a project as well!

The portfolio had the main goals of showcasing who I am, what I know, and what I have worked on. While a portfolio may not serve much use as a student as of now, I hope to continue improving it such that it will be ready to go when the time comes for me to use it.

My portfolio has certainly undergone many changes, but each of them was intentional in a way! Each change represents a change as I continue developing as an individual and developer. While content may change, I hope that the main message behind them do not.

The current iteration of the portfolio is version 2. Version 3 is coming up and in the works; compared to any other version, version 3 is built from the ground up!
